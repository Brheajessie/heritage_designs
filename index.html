WE ARE HERITAGE DESIGNS

Proudly handmade in Louisville, Kentucky since 2018. Custom pieces for your life now, and years to come.


Chris and Melissa Jessie make each Heritage Designs piece in their Louisville, Kentucky home. They got into the world of custom furniture making many years ago after upcycling thrifted pieces and breathing new life into them. In 2017 Chris and Melissa made the leap to chase their dream that soon became Heritage Designs. Each piece is one of a kind and has it's own story. These pieces are made with love and to be enjoyed with friends and family. Heritage Designs furniture are converstaion pieces for any home and something you can take pride in owning.
